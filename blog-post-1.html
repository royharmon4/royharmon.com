What We’ll Cover

This guide explains what a go-to-market strategy is, outlines common approaches, and helps you choose the right path for your startup.

What Is a GTM Strategy?

A go-to-market strategy is the plan for how your company reaches buyers, communicates value, and turns demand into revenue.

Common GTM Strategies

Product-led, sales-led, marketing-led, and ecosystem-led approaches each fit different products and stages of growth.

How to Choose the Right Strategy

Start with the customer, your product complexity, and the motion your team can support consistently.

A strong GTM strategy reduces guesswork by turning positioning, channels, and execution into one coherent plan.
